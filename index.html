imbibo

comprehensive cocktail database

If you are looking to looking for a rare cocktail find, or looking for a recipe to make your favorite cocktail at home, look no furthre than imbibo

Search the ingredient or cocktail name, to find a list of cocktail that contains the ingredient, or a detailed recipe with ingredients and instruction of the cocktail.

Implemented with Semantic UI: fully responsive for your phone, tablet, and desktop, so that you can take it and use it anywhere.

Implemented with Yelp: search for bars nearby, with yelp rating, contact information, and the distance.
